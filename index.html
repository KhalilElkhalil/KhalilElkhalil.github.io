Interests

Apart from research, I enjoy watching movies and reading books. Now, I am reading a book that has to some extent changed the way I look to the world, it is called "Sapiens" a masterpiece of the legendary Yuval Noah Harari. Although, it seems intimidating to most people, it studies the human history from a completely rational view. I don't want to spoil the book, so it is for you to go and read it.

I also like watching documentaries from time to time. I still remember the first documentary I watched: "Einstein's big idea" broadcasted in 2005 celebrating 100 years after Einstein's special relativity. It is still a good one, go and watch it if you didn't; you will discover many other briliant scientists such as Faraday, Lavoisier, Lise Meitner and Emilie du Chatelet. This is how I developed a passion for science.
